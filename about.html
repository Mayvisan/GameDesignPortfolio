About Me

Hi! I'm Isabella, a video game designer passionate about crafting narrative experiences and immersive worlds that emotionally connect with players. Through participating in Game Jams, I’ve explored design, art direction, narrative, and occasional programming roles, gaining valuable skills in quick decision-making, teamwork, and translating ideas into engaging mechanics.

My focus as a designer is storytelling and creating impactful themes. I enjoy designing deep characters and alternate realities while experimenting with mechanics that challenge perceptions. My projects often explore topics like time, loneliness, and psychological struggles, inspired by genres such as psychological horror and narrative RPGs.

I'm building my portfolio with creative and innovative projects, aiming to grow as a designer and contribute fresh perspectives to the video game industry. If you'd like to connect or discuss ideas, feel free to reach out. Thanks for stopping by! :)
